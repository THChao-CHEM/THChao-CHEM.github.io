About

Built for hiring managers first.

I am a PhD researcher working at the intersection of computational chemistry, materials design, biomolecular machine learning, and mechanochemistry. My work focuses on using models and molecular insight to accelerate experimental decision-making and reduce search space in complex chemical systems.

This website highlights research that translates well to industrial environments: predictive modeling, scalable workflows, interpretable analysis, and rigorous communication of technical results.

What industry teams usually care about

  • Can you work across chemistry, data, and engineering?
